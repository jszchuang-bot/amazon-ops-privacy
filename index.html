Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

Overview

Amazon Ops MVP is an internal tool used by our authorized team to access Amazon Ads reporting and campaign performance data for advertising accounts that we own or are authorized to manage.

Data We Access

With authorization, this tool may access Amazon Ads reporting data, campaign performance data, search term data, keyword and targeting data, and related advertising account profile information.

How We Use Data

We use the data only for advertising reporting, campaign diagnostics, performance analysis, operational decision support, and internal business management.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share Amazon Ads data with third parties. We do not use the data for unrelated advertising, resale, or unauthorized profiling.

Data Security

Access tokens, refresh tokens, client secrets, and advertising data are stored securely and are not published publicly. Access is limited to authorized personnel only.
